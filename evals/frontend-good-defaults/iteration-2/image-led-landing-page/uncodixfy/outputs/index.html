Rook House

Ten quiet rooms in Cedofeita, with granite stairs, Portuguese oak, and breakfast served in the courtyard before the city warms up.

A restored merchant house, kept small on purpose.

Rook House sits between Rua de Cedofeita galleries and the old clerks' cafes near Carmo. Rooms are simple and tactile: linen curtains, cork floors, hand-thrown lamps, and windows that open to tiled roofs.

Rooms ready for late trains and slow mornings.

Each room is booked directly with the house. Rates include breakfast, filtered water, and a printed Porto map marked by the staff.
