A restored merchant house, not a hotel block.

Rook House sits on the quieter end of Rua das Flores, close enough to walk everywhere and tucked far enough back for sleep. The entrance opens from granite pavement into a compact lobby, then through to a tiled courtyard where breakfast runs late.

The team keeps a short local list: the bakery that still has broa at 10:30, the wine bar that answers the phone, and the tram stop worth skipping when the queue wraps around the corner.
