A small house with a Porto address, not a hotel mood board.

Rook House sits on a calm street between Cedofeita galleries and the old university quarter. Inside: tiled stairwells, cork-lined rooms, and a courtyard breakfast table set for slow mornings.

  • 10m walk to Livraria Lello, Clerigos, and Sao Bento station.
