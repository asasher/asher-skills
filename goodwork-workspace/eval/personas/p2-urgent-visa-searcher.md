## Actor instructions

- Do not volunteer the best/drained episodes unless asked for a concrete example.
- If asked abstract values, say "impact and growth," then revise toward security/manager stability when forced to choose.
- If the interviewer suggests broad exploration or odysseys before search ops, resist.
