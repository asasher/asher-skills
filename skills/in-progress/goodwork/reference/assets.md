# assets — the outward surface: CV, LinkedIn, portfolio, proof of work

Build what the market sees, from the profile and niche. Strategy lives here; document mechanics delegate.

## Delegation

If the `eloquent` skill is available, use it for document production: `compile`, `tailor`, `ats`, `cover-letter`, `linkedin`, `portfolio-case-study`, feeding it the Good Work Profile and `NICHE.md` — verified evidence instead of asking cold. Without eloquent, produce the documents directly under the same rules: never invent experience, employers, metrics, or credentials; label inference as positioning, not fact.

## What goodwork adds on top of documents

**Narrative coherence.** Every asset tells the same story as the niche two-pager: who they are, the intersection they own, the evidence. The LinkedIn headline states the target identity (not "open to work"; not the old identity). For career changers, the summary narrates the bridge — what carries over and why the move makes sense.

**The proof-of-work plan.** Documents claim; artifacts demonstrate. Pick the proof format the target market actually inspects:
- Engineering: PRs to relevant open source, a working demo, a technical teardown.
- Product/GTM/strategy: a published teardown of a target-adjacent product, a market map, a case study.
- Design: case studies showing process, not just outcomes.
- Writing/advisory: essays, a talk at the community meetup, a public learning log.

Rules: sustained and relevant beats scattered (one repo with real commits over ten toys); each artifact should make an insider say "this person has clearly thought about this"; cadence one artifact per strategic niche every 1–2 weeks during an active search. Add each planned artifact to `daily` as a scheduled action.

**Evidence-gating.** Tailoring only helps when real overlap exists. If the profile lacks evidence for a target's requirements, the fix is a prototype or proof artifact that creates the evidence — not adjectives. Flag gaps honestly: "you have no story for X; here's the two-week artifact that would create one."

**Consistency check.** Before the campaign: resume, LinkedIn, portfolio, community bios, and the two-pager all name the same niche, same headline strengths, same proof links. Mismatched surfaces read as unfocused.

## Output

Updated documents (via eloquent where available), a proof-of-work plan appended to `goodwork/NICHE.md` with the artifact queue, the consistency audit result. Nothing publishes without the user's explicit approval of the final text.
