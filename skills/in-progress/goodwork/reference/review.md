# review - the weekly loop

Thirty minutes, once a week, same day. Closes the loop between the campaign (what happened) and the profile (what it means). Reads `pipeline.json`, `targets.json`, `leads.json`, `metrics.json`, `evidence-inbox.json`, `JOURNAL.md`, `EXPERIMENTS.md`, `NICHE.md`, `PROFILE.md`.

## 1. The numbers (5 min)

Pull the week's metrics: outreach sent, response rate, conversations, referrals, applications, screens, interviews, offers, source-of-interview. Compare to quota and last week. Run the funnel diagnosis from [pipeline.md](pipeline.md) — name the narrowest constriction and which command fixes it. One diagnosis per week; changing three variables at once makes next week's numbers meaningless.

## 2. The energy read (5 min)

Scan the journal week: real wins, consistent energizers/drainers, where flow showed up. Two questions:
- Does the pattern match the profile's energy map? Contradictions are findings — route to `profile`.
- Is the *search design* fitting the person? (Drowning in hated cold outreach while the community route sits unused is a design bug, not a discipline problem.)

## 3. The learning read (10 min)

- Conversation debriefs: is the market confirming or correcting the niche hypotheses? Every ~5 conversations `NICHE.md` should visibly update. Unchanged through three active weeks → either converged (say so) or debriefs aren't being mined.
- Experiments: which prototypes hit their time box? Debrief per [prototype.md](prototype.md), move claims to *tested*, kill or escalate each explicitly.
- Reason codes on the week's rejections/retirements: three of the same code is a message.
- Drain `evidence-inbox.json` into `PROFILE.md` per [profile.md](profile.md). Don't leave profile-relevant evidence stranded.

## 4. Recommit or redesign (10 min)

- Set next week's quotas — tuned to the diagnosis, real capacity, honest urgency. Shrinking deliberately is fine; drifting is not.
- Refill triggers: bench targets, artifact queue, conversation list.
- **Monthly, additionally**: re-score the active odyssey plan's gauges; check the whole-life dashboard (what has the search been costing?); ask "If I were starting this search fresh today, knowing what I know, would I design it the same way?" If no: change it, log why in the profile changelog.
- **On offer or search-end**: full debrief into the profile — what the search proved about this person's good work. The profile outlives the search; suggest a light `daily` journal habit for the first 90 days of the new role, feeding a six-month `profile` review.

## Output

A short written review appended to `goodwork/JOURNAL.md` (numbers, diagnosis, energy read, evidence decisions, learnings, next week's plan), profile/niche updates applied, `metrics.json` and `evidence-inbox.json` updated, and the single most important change for next week in one sentence.
