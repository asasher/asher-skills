# outreach — conversations that carry the search

Referrals and weak ties carry most opportunity; portals carry volume. This command runs the human layer: the listening tour, informational interviews, referral asks, cold outreach, and follow-up cadence. Nothing sends without the user's approval of the final text.

## The listening tour (before asking for anything)

Early in a search or pivot, run 15–25 conversations whose goal is **market truth, not jobs**: former managers and peers, recruiters, role incumbents, hiring managers, community operators. Capture per call: the market's language for the user's value, repeated objections, skill gaps named, realistic titles and salary ranges, and "who else should I talk to?" (the compounding question). After every ~5 calls, update the niche hypotheses in `NICHE.md` — if the market keeps correcting the positioning, the positioning changes, not the market.

## Getting the meeting

Prioritize: warm intros > community relationships > weak ties (ex-colleagues, alumni, acquaintances — often fresher information than close friends) > cold. Cold outreach template skeleton:

> Subject: Quick question from [shared context]
> Hi [Name] — I'm researching [role/field] and noticed [specific, true reason: their post, talk, project]. Could I ask for 20 minutes about your path and what you're seeing in the market? I'm not asking for a job — I'm trying to focus my search properly.

Rules: one specific true compliment-of-substance, one clear small ask, no CV attached, no "pick your brain," never mass-personalized spam. Every message individually reviewed — over-automation reads instantly and burns targets.

## Running the conversation (TIARA)

20–30 minutes, ~10 prepared questions, research done beforehand. Arc: **T**rends ("what's changing in the field?") → **I**nsights ("what surprises people about this work?") → **A**dvice ("what would you do in my position?") → **R**esources ("what should I read/join?") → **A**ssignments ("what small project would prove readiness?"). The arc moves them from commentator to advisor — people who advise you become invested in you. Do not ask for a job or referral in the first conversation; referral asks come after a real relationship exists, and only with permission to use their name.

## Follow-up cadence (3B7, pre-decided so anxiety doesn't improvise)

- No reply → follow up after **3 business days** → again **~7 days** later → then archive to monthly low-touch nurture. Never more.
- After a conversation: thank-you within 24–72 hours, send anything promised, report back outcomes later ("took your advice, here's what happened" — the most underused relationship builder).
- Log every touch in `PIPELINE.md` with a next-action date; `daily` surfaces what's due.

## Quotas (defaults, tune in `review`)

3–5 conversations/week during active search; 10–25 outreach messages/week depending on urgency; 2–3 follow-up blocks/week. Watch response rate: under ~15% on warmish outreach means the message or the targeting is off — diagnose in `review` before sending more.

## Output

Drafts for approval, per-conversation prep sheets (their background, why-them, the 10 questions), call debriefs appended to `goodwork/PIPELINE.md`, and niche-hypothesis updates flagged for `NICHE.md`.
