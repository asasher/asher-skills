# daily - today's actions and the journal

The sustain loop, run at the start (queue) and end (journal) of a working day. Keep it to minutes: this is a heartbeat, not a meeting. Progress on meaningful work is the strongest daily morale driver (Amabile) — the queue exists to manufacture a visible small win every single day, because searches die of momentum loss more than rejection.

## First - reconcile

Run [reconcile.md](reconcile.md) first whenever `capabilities.json` records `manual` requested now, `on-demand`, or a due scheduled cadence. Inbound replies change today's queue before new outbound work is chosen.

## Morning — the action queue

Build today's list from `pipeline.json`, `leads.json`, `targets.json`, `metrics.json`, and open narrative commitments, smallest sufficient set (default 3–5 items, ≤90 minutes total unless the user wants a heavy day):

1. **Due follow-ups** from `pipeline.json` (the 3-day/7-day cadence and promised thank-yous) — these are commitments, they go first.
2. **One pipeline-advancing action**: an outreach message, a conversation prep, a high-fit application step.
3. **One compounding action**: proof-of-work artifact progress, community participation (answer a question, share something useful), or a prototype step from `EXPERIMENTS.md`.
4. **Refill work only if a trigger fired** (bench below 10 targets, lead bench stale, artifact queue empty).

Each item: concrete, time-boxed, with its pipeline row named. If the user is low-energy, shrink the queue rather than skip the day — one follow-up sent keeps the streak alive. Never generate busywork to fill the quota; an honest 2-item day beats padding.

## Evening — the Good Time Journal (60 seconds)

Three lines in `goodwork/JOURNAL.md`:

```
2026-07-03 | did: [what actually happened] | win: [smallest real progress] | energy: [what energized / what drained, one phrase each] | flow: [y/n, during what]
```

This is not a diary; it's instrumentation. The energy/flow fields feed `review`'s pattern detection and keep testing the profile's energy map against reality — including the search activities themselves (someone who consistently lights up during informational interviews and dreads solo artifact work is learning something about their next role).

## Cadence flags (raise, don't nag)

- 3+ days with no journal entry → ask once whether to shrink the daily footprint or pause the campaign honestly (a declared pause beats a silent fade).
- A week of completed queues with zero energy-positive entries → flag for `review`: the search design itself may be misfit (too much cold outreach for this person, not enough community route — or vice versa).
- Streaks get named plainly ("12 straight days") — momentum is a real asset; don't manufacture fake celebration around it.

## Output

Today's queue (with pipeline IDs), `pipeline.json` next actions updated, tonight's journal line appended, and any cadence flags routed to `review`.
