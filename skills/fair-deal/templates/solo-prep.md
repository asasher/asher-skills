# Solo Prep — PRIVATE (never committed)

This file lives only on your machine (it's gitignored). It holds the few things that **never go on the
table** — so your agent can hold them for you in the room without inventing them under pressure. Your agent
reads this to advocate for you; it will **never** copy anything here into a committed file, and before any move
that would cross your floor it will stop and check with you first.

> The standing habit: *"I agree the structure through my agent, but any new number comes back to this sheet
> first. 'Let me sleep on it' is a complete sentence."*

## 1 · My true goals & concerns
- What I actually want out of this deal (the real motivation, not the polite version): ____
- What I'm quietly worried about / what would make me resent this in a year: ____
- "I win when ___": ____

## 2 · My floor (private)
- The lowest overall shape I'd still sign: ____
- 2–3 things I will NOT concede whatever happens (e.g. my pre-existing IP stays mine · I keep a data export ·
  there's a written way out): ____
- My walk-away — what I do instead if it's below the floor (this is my real leverage; calm, not a threat): ____

## 3 · My benchmarks (private)
- The objective number behind each ask, so it isn't "what feels fair to a friend" (market salary · agency
  margin · SaaS price · royalty range · capital-at-risk multiple): ____
- An ambitious-but-reasonable opener: ____

## 4 · Notes my agent should hold
- Anything else my representative should know but not reveal: ____
