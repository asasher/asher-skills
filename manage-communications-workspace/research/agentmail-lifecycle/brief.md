**Question.** Can the planned successor to `manage-communications` use AgentMail alone to send approved stakeholder email, record delivery lifecycle, and associate replies with the original communication?

**Intended use.** Bound the delivery and tracking architecture in the Relay skill plan; this is not an implementation guide or a guarantee of deployment configuration.

**Sufficient answer.** Establish whether each required lifecycle event and correlation identifier is documented, and identify what the repository must provide for durable automatic event intake.
