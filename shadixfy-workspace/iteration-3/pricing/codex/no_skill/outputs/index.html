Questions

Billing basics

Can I switch later?

Yes. Move between monthly and annual billing from workspace settings. Plan limits update immediately.

Do previews count as projects?

No. Projects are repositories or services. Previews are temporary environments created from branches.

What happens at the limit?

Existing deployments keep running. New previews pause until you archive old ones or upgrade.

Is there a startup discount?

Early-stage teams can request credits for the Team plan after completing identity verification.
