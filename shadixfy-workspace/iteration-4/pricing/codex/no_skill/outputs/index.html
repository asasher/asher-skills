FAQ

Short answers.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrades apply immediately, and downgrades take effect at the next billing date.

Do you charge for services?

No on paid plans. Pro and Team can connect unlimited apps, APIs, workers, and queues.

Is annual billing required?

No. Monthly billing is available for every paid plan, with annual billing discounted.

Can Team be invoiced?

Yes. Team supports invoicing, purchase orders, SSO setup, and procurement reviews.
